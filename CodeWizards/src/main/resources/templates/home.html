About

Together, we create magic...
This coding club is by the students and for the students. The club primarily focuses on 3 aspects

  1. Learning - The learning phase is taken care by some mentors group from the students. It may last for few days. It primarily focuses on learning a technology or a language.
  2. Building - Having a purpose behind is the first thing and the fun follows. Building is the phase where People start creating stuff. And building things is the purpose.
  3. Exploring - A phase of freedom. The coding club will be active in the college. The students can take part in coding challenges and hackathons. Now it is their part to take forward their learning and help junior coders.
